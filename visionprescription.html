4.29 Resource VisionPrescription - Content

This resource cultivated by the Financial Management Work Group

An authorization for the supply of glasses and/or contact lenses to a patient.

4.29.1 Scope and Usage

This resource covers all prescriptions for glasses and contact lenses for a patient.

4.29.2 Boundaries and Relationships

This resource is referenced by [VisionClaim]
