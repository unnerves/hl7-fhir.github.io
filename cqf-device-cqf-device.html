Profile: cqf-device

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/Profile/cqf-device-cqf-device

. Profile of Device for decision support/quality metrics. Indicates which elements must be supported by CQF rules and measure engines. : Clinical Quality Framework (CQF) Device Profile
