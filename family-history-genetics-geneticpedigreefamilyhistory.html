Profile: GeneticPedigreeFamilyHistory

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/Profile/family-history-genetics-geneticpedigreefamilyhistory

. Adds additional information to a family history supporting both the capture of mother/father relationships as well as additional observations necessary to enable genetics-based risk analysis for patients : Family history for genetics analysis

This profile was published on Wed, Oct 2, 2013 00:00+0000 as a draft by HL7 International - Clinical Genomics WG.
